Spicy Mango Mezcal Margarita

This Mango Mezcal Margarita is sweet and spicy at the same time, with the sweetness of mango juice and the bite of cayenne pepper. Usually a margarita has tequila in it, along with lime juice and orange liqueur like Cointreau.

This cocktail strays a bit from the norm. We love Mezcal and are always looking for new ways to enjoy it. So this Mango Margarita uses mezcal instead of tequila. This spicy Mango Mezcal Margarita is a fun twist on a classic, with sweet and fruity mango, smoky Mezcal and a touch of heat.

Ingredients

  • 1 teaspoon spicy rim salt
  • x2.5 ounces mango juice
  • ounces freshly squeezed lime juice
  • 1.5 ounces mezcal
  • 1 teaspoon agave syrup
  • 1/8 teaspoon cayenne pepper

Steps

  1. Pour the rim salt onto a flat plate. Run a lime wedge around the rim of your preferred glass.
  2. Dip the rim into rim salt mixed with a sprinkle of cayenne pepper.
  3. Mix the mango juice, lime juice, mezcal, agave syrup, and one shake of cayenne pepper, with ice in a cocktail shaker. Shake vigorously until well chilled.
  4. Pour the cocktail into the prepared glass, with ice. Garnish with fresh mango.
